# Privacy Policy

## 1. Data Collection
This application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data.

## 2. Video and Location Data
All video files and GPS/location data processed by this application are handled locally on the user's device. No data is uploaded to any external servers or shared with third parties.

## 3. Third-Party Services
This application may use map tile providers (e.g., OpenStreetMap) to display the map. These services may receive your IP address for the purpose of serving map data, but no personal identification is linked.

## 4. Contact
For questions regarding this App, please contact the developer.
